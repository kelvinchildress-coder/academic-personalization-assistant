Why Guides Use This

  • Pull student data fast — No more clicking through TimeBack one student at a time. Get XP, accuracy, and minutes for your entire roster in seconds.
  • See the full picture — View daily or weekly breakdowns with leaderboards, trends, and color-coded flags so you know exactly who needs attention.
  • Track progress throughout the day — Pull fresh data anytime to see real-time student activity as they work.
  • Organize by groups — Create custom groups to filter by guide group, grade level, or any way you work with your students.
